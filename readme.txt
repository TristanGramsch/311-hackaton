We have built a production-ready voice AI agent on Vapi that answers 311 non-emergency calls, classifies requests, routes them to the correct city department, and logs Service Requests (SRs) directly into 311.boston.gov

U.S. cities field an estimated 162–804 million non-emergency calls per year (gross estimation, as no official resource is available), at an average handling cost of 4.00–5.00 per call. Call centers are simultaneously losing staff to retirement and poor conditions, while residents expect private-sector response quality. There is no market leader in 311 modernization — Salesforce, Microsoft, and local consultancies deliver fragmented custom builds. Our voice agent resolves the cost, staffing, and quality problem in a single deployable package, and it slots into the CRMs cities already own.
