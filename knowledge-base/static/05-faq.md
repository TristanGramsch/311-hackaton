# Boston 311 Frequently Asked Questions

## What is 311?
311 is a free, easy-to-remember phone number that connects Boston residents with trained constituent service center representatives for non-emergency city services and information.

## When is 311 available?
24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

## What's the difference between 311 and 911?
- **311**: Non-emergency city services and information (potholes, trash, permits, etc.)
- **911**: Emergencies only (fire, crime in progress, medical emergency)

## How do I contact 311?
- **Phone**: Dial 3-1-1 from within Boston, or (617) 635-4500 from anywhere
- **App**: Download BOS:311 from the App Store or Google Play
- **Online**: https://311.boston.gov/
- **Email**: 311@boston.gov
- **In person**: 1 City Hall Square, Boston, MA 02201, Mayor's Office 5th Floor (weekdays 9am-5pm)

## Can I call 311 from a cell phone?
Yes. If you can't connect, dial (617) 635-4500 instead. Standard airtime charges apply.

## Can I call 311 from a VoIP phone?
It depends on your VoIP provider. Contact them to ensure 311 is configured. If it doesn't work, use (617) 635-4500.

## Why can't I get through dialing 311 from my office?
Some office phone systems need configuration to route 311 calls. Contact your telecom office, or use (617) 635-4500 as an alternative.

## How do I track my service request?
After submitting a request, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Monitor your case at https://311.boston.gov/.
- **New system cases**: Start with "BCS-"
- **Legacy cases**: 12-digit numbers

## What if my case number starts with "BCS-"?
Search for it at: https://data.boston.gov/dataset/311-service-requests/resource/254adca6-64ab-4c5c-9fc0-a6da622be185

## What if my case number is a 12-digit number?
Search for it at: https://data.boston.gov/dataset/311-service-requests/resource/1a0b420d-99f1-4887-9851-990b2a5a6e17

## What is the BOS:311 app?
A free mobile app for reporting non-emergency issues like potholes and graffiti. You can upload photos and track requests.
- **iOS**: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bos-311/id330894558
- **Android**: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=gov.cityofboston.citizensconnect

## Is there a data transition happening?
Yes. Starting October 2025, 311 service requests are transitioning to a new backend system. During the transition (expected through mid-2026), some cases appear in the legacy dataset and some in the new system dataset. Contact doitbcstech@boston.gov with questions.

## What is my trash pickup day?
Look up your schedule at: boston.gov/departments/public-works/trash-and-recycling-day-schedule-and-search

## How do holidays affect trash collection?
When a holiday falls on or before your collection day, pickup shifts to one day later for the rest of that week.

## Where can I find historical 311 data?
At https://data.boston.gov/dataset/311-service-requests — data is available from 2011 to present, organized by year.
