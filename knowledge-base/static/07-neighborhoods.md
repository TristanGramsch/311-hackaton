## Notes
- Dorchester is the largest neighborhood by area and population.
- "South Boston Waterfront" is also commonly called "the Seaport."
- Some datasets use slightly different neighborhood names; match flexibly.
- If a caller provides an address outside Boston city limits, politely inform them that Boston 311 serves only the City of Boston and suggest they contact their own municipality's services.
