# Department Routing Guide

## Department Directory

### Public Works Department (PWD)
- **Handles**: Potholes, sidewalk repair, street cleaning, trash/recycling collection, missed pickups, bulk item collection, illegal dumping, improper trash storage, street light outages, snow removal
- **Code in legacy system**: PWDx
- **New system name**: Public Works Department (PWD)

### Inspectional Services Department (ISD)
- **Handles**: Building permits, building/housing inspections, code enforcement, zoning violations, illegal occupancy, poor property conditions, construction complaints, lead paint, overcrowding, heat complaints, unsatisfactory living conditions
- **Code in legacy system**: ISD
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/inspectional-services

### Boston Transportation Department (BTD)
- **Handles**: Traffic signs and signals, parking enforcement, abandoned vehicles, pavement markings, crosswalks, street occupancy permits, bike infrastructure
- **Code in legacy system**: BTDT
- **New system name**: Transportation

### Parks & Recreation
- **Handles**: Park maintenance, play equipment, fountains, park lights, tree planting/pruning/removal, cemetery maintenance, athletic permits, event permits in parks
- **New system name**: Parks & Recreation

### Animal Care & Control
- **Handles**: Lost pets, domestic animal issues, wild animal issues, animal bites, barking dogs, dead animal pickup, dog licensing
- **New system name**: Animal Care & Control
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/animal-care-and-control

### Boston Police Department (BPD)
- **Handles**: Noise complaints (parties), taxi complaints, crime tips, non-emergency police matters
- **Note**: For emergencies, always direct to 911
- **Website**: police.boston.gov

### Boston Fire Department (BFD)
- **Handles**: Fire prevention permits, smoke/CO detector inspections, fire safety
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/fire-operations

### Boston Public Health Commission (BPHC)
- **Handles**: Restaurant inspections, food safety, health alerts, asthma, lead poisoning prevention, substance abuse
- **Website**: boston.gov/government/cabinets/boston-public-health-commission

### Environment Department
- **Handles**: Energy, sustainability, climate, fuel resources
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/environment

### Licensing Board
- **Handles**: Alcohol licenses, entertainment licenses, common victualler, lodging/dormitory licenses
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/licensing-board

### Consumer Affairs and Licensing
- **Handles**: Public event permits, consumer complaints, weights and measures, carnival licenses
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/consumer-affairs

### Assessing Department
- **Handles**: Property valuations, tax assessments, address changes for tax bills
- **Website**: cityofboston.gov/assessing/search/

### Parking Clerk
- **Handles**: Parking tickets (payment, appeals, notifications), resident parking permits
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/parking-clerk

### Registry (Birth, Death, Marriage)
- **Handles**: Vital records — birth, death, marriage certificates
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/registry-birth-death-and-marriage

### Election Department
- **Handles**: Voter registration, absentee ballots, election information
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/election

### Neighborhood Services
- **Handles**: Neighborhood coordination, community engagement
- **Website**: boston.gov/departments/neighborhood-services
