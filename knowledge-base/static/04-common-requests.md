# Common 311 Requests — Step-by-Step

## 1. Report a Pothole
- **Department**: Public Works (PWD)
- **Steps**: Submit online at https://311.boston.gov/ or call 311. Provide the street address or nearest intersection and describe the size/depth if possible. A photo helps.
- **SLA**: Typically addressed within 1-3 business days depending on severity.

## 2. Missed Trash or Recycling Pickup
- **Department**: PWD
- **Steps**: Report at http://mayors24.boston.gov/Ef3/SSP_PWDx02_MissedTrashRecyclingYardWasteBulkItem.xml or call 311. Provide your address. Leave bins curbside until collected.
- **Note**: Check your trash day schedule at boston.gov/departments/public-works/trash-and-recycling-day-schedule-and-search. Holidays shift collection by one day.

## 3. Bulk Item / Special Collection Pickup
- **Department**: PWD
- **Steps**: Visit boston.gov/departments/public-works/special-collection-items or call 311. Items include TVs, fridges, ACs, water coolers, dehumidifiers, mattresses.
- **Note**: Freon-containing appliances (fridges, ACs) require scheduling. Leave items curbside on your trash day with a note visible.

## 4. Report a Streetlight Outage
- **Department**: PWD (city-maintained) or Eversource (utility-maintained)
- **Steps**: Submit at https://311.boston.gov/ or call 311. Provide the pole number (stamped on the pole) and street address.
- **Note**: PWD maintains ~40,000 of the city's 67,000 streetlights and contracts out the rest.

## 5. Request Graffiti Removal
- **Department**: PWD / Property Management
- **Steps**: Submit at https://311.boston.gov/ or via BOS:311 app. Provide address and photo. City-owned property is cleaned directly. Private property requires owner agreement.
- **Link**: boston.gov/departments/property-management/cleaning-graffiti-city-boston

## 6. Report Illegal Dumping
- **Department**: PWD Code Enforcement
- **Steps**: Submit via BOS:311 app or online. Provide exact location and photos if possible.

## 7. Request Street Cleaning
- **Department**: PWD
- **Steps**: Submit via BOS:311 app or call 311. Provide the street and cross street.
- **Schedules**: cityofboston.gov/publicworks/sweeping/

## 8. Report a Noise Complaint (Loud Party)
- **Department**: Boston Police (non-emergency)
- **Steps**: Report at police.boston.gov/report-loud-parties/ or call 311. Provide the address and nature of the noise.
- **Note**: For ongoing disturbances posing safety risk, call 911.

## 9. Report an Abandoned Vehicle
- **Department**: BTD Enforcement
- **Steps**: Submit via BOS:311 app or call 311. Provide the vehicle description (make, model, color), license plate if visible, and location.

## 10. Pay or Appeal a Parking Ticket
- **Pay**: boston.gov/departments/parking-clerk/how-pay-parking-ticket
- **Appeal**: boston.gov/departments/parking-clerk/how-appeal-parking-ticket
- **Notifications**: cityofboston.gov/parking/portal.asp

## 11. Get a Resident Parking Permit
- **Department**: Parking Clerk
- **Steps**: Apply at boston.gov/departments/parking-clerk/how-get-resident-parking-permit. Need proof of residency and vehicle registration.

## 12. Report a Housing/Building Code Violation
- **Department**: ISD
- **Steps**: Call 311 or submit online. Describe the violation (poor conditions, no heat, overcrowding, illegal occupancy, etc.). An inspector will be assigned.
- **Heat complaints**: Landlords must maintain 68°F during day (64°F at night) from Sep 15 - Jun 15.

## 13. Request a Tree Service (Planting, Pruning, Removal)
- **Department**: Parks & Recreation
- **Steps**: Submit at https://311.boston.gov/ or call 311. Specify whether it's a planting request, pruning, or removal, and provide the address.

## 14. Report a Lost Pet
- **Department**: Animal Care & Control
- **Steps**: Submit at https://311.boston.gov/. Provide a description, last known location, and photo. Also check the shelter: boston.gov/departments/animal-care-and-control/animal-care-and-control-shelter

## 15. Needle Cleanup Request
- **Department**: Mayor's 24-Hour Hotline / Needle Program
- **Steps**: Submit via BOS:311 app or call 311. Provide the exact location. Do not handle needles yourself.

## 16. Report a Traffic Signal or Sign Problem
- **Department**: BTD Signs & Signals
- **Steps**: Submit via BOS:311 app or call 311. Describe the malfunction and location.
