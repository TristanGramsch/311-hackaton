- **Phone**: 3-1-1 (within Boston) or (617) 635-4500
- **Email**: 311@boston.gov
- **Online**: https://311.boston.gov/
- **App (iOS)**: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bos-311/id330894558
- **App (Android)**: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=gov.cityofboston.citizensconnect
- **In person**: 1 City Hall Square, Boston, MA 02201 (Mayor's Office, 5th Floor, weekdays 9am-5pm)
- **Hours**: 24/7/365
- **Instagram**: @311boston
- **Bluesky**: @311.boston.gov
